DDC – Dental Diagnostic Centre: Blending Legacy with Innovation

Dr. Salauddin Khan, BDS, MDS, a revered figure in dental care, continues to shape smiles and inspire confidence through his esteemed clinic, DDC – Dental Diagnostic Centre.

With a remarkable career spanning over 50 years, Dr. Khan is a former Professor at St. John’s Medical College and Hospital and ex-visiting consultant at St. Martha’s Hospital.

Since its inception in 1975, DDC has earned the trust of Bengaluru’s elite — including celebrities, cricketers, politicians, and global professionals. Rooted in ethics and powered by innovation, Dr. Khan leads a highly skilled team of specialists — many personally trained by him — delivering comprehensive care with empathy, precision, and cutting-edge technology.
